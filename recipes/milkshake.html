Milkshake Recipe

Milkshake YumYum

Perfect Milkshake!

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredient

  • Milk
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Malt Powder
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Scoop 4 scoops of vanilla ice cream into your blender or milkshake maker
  2. Pour milk so it is nearly covering all ice cream
  3. Add as much chocolate syrup as preferred
  4. Add two heaping scoops of malt power. Or don't if you are lame.
  5. Blend together until smooth consistency is achieved
  6. Pour into glass and add whipped cream on top
  7. Enjoy!
