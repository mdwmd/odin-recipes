Ribeye Recipe

Easy Cast-Iron Ribeye

This is an easy indoor recipe for a ribeye steak using a cast-iron pan.

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 Minutes

Ingredient

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Steak Seasoning
  • Worshecshtheritiitiere Sauce
  • Butter

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Rub Worsheshtercshitestr sauce into the steak
  3. Generously season with your choice of steak seasoning
  4. Heat cast-iron pan on medium-high heat on stovetop
  5. When pan is hot put in 1/8 tbs of butter
  6. Sear steak for 1 min on each side in pan on stove top, basting with butter
  7. place castiron pan with seared ribeye into the overn for 8 minutes
  8. Remove pan from oven and place steak on resting tray and let reast 5 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
